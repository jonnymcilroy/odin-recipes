Tortilla Pizza

Description

Tortilla pizza is a quick and easy dish where a tortilla serves as a thin, crispy crust. Itâ€™s made by topping a large tortilla with sauce, cheese, and various toppings, then baking or pan-frying until the edges are golden and the cheese is melted. This simple, versatile recipe is perfect for a personal-sized pizza that cooks in minutes, making it a convenient option for busy days or casual meals. Tortilla pizzas can be customized with favorite ingredients, from classic pepperoni and veggies to more creative options like pesto and fresh herbs.

Ingredients

  • Tortilla
  • Oil
  • Seasonings
  • Tomato sauce
  • Toppings
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Brush the tortilla with olive oil, season, and bake.
  2. Top with tomato sauce and toppings. Sprinkle it with cheese.
  3. Bake again until the cheese is melted, then season it with oregano.
